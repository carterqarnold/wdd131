Project Siteplan

Site Name

Understanding Dancing

I chose this name because it summarizes best what my website is about and what it is meant to do

Site Purpose

my website is going to have sources that can help you understand dancing, possible places you can go to learn such as camps, clubs, and classes, and where to go to get the videos to learn online, along with a contact form for possible instructors for online learning.

Scenarios

How much is it for a dance instructor? Can I have a specific instructor that best suits my learning style? how do I meet with the instructor and what equipment will I need?

Color Schema

rgb(204, 6, 22) is the background color of the header and the footer, white is the color of the text for the header and footer, and rgb(156, 28, 160) for the background when hovering over the navigation links.

Typograghy

The Lora font for h1, h2 and h3 and the Roboto font for the paragraphs
